Instructions

Welcome to SpeedType.io, the ultimate typing test to challenge your typing speed and accuracy. Start the test, type the text as quickly and accurately as you can, and see your results!

  • Choose your desired difficulty level and test duration from the dropdown menus.
  • Click the "Start Test" button to begin.
  • Type the displayed text as quickly and accurately as you can.
  • Your typing speed (WPM) and accuracy will be calculated in real-time.
  • The test will end when you complete the text or when the timer reaches zero.
  • View your final results and share them on social media if you'd like!
  • Click "Restart Test" to try again with a new text passage.

Good luck, and happy typing!
