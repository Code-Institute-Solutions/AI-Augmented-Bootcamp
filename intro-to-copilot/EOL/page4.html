Butter Chicken

A popular Indian dish made with tender chicken cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce.

Ingredients:
  1. Chicken
  2. Butter
  3. Onion
  4. Garlic
  5. Ginger
  6. Tomatoes
  7. Heavy cream
  8. Spices (such as garam masala, turmeric, and chili powder)
  9. Salt
  10. Cilantro (for garnish)
Cooking Instructions:
  1. Heat butter in a pan and sauté onions, garlic, and ginger until golden.
  2. Add chicken pieces and brown them on all sides.
  3. Introduce tomatoes and spices, cooking until the tomatoes are soft.
  4. Pour in heavy cream and simmer until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Adjust the seasoning with salt, and garnish with cilantro before serving.
  6. Enjoy with rice or naan bread.
Chole Bhature

A popular North Indian dish made with spicy chickpea curry (chole) and deep-fried bread (bhature).

Ingredients:
  1. Chickpeas
  2. Onion
  3. Tomatoes
  4. Ginger
  5. Garlic
  6. Green chilies
  7. Spices (such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala)
  8. Oil
  9. Baking soda
  10. All-purpose flour
  11. Yogurt
  12. Salt
Cooking Instructions:
  1. Begin by soaking the chickpeas overnight in water. Drain and rinse, then boil in fresh water until tender.
  2. In a large pan, heat oil over medium heat. Add finely chopped onions, minced ginger, garlic, and chopped green chilies. Sauté until the onions are golden brown.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and oil begins to separate. Stir in the spices: cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala. Cook for a few minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the boiled chickpeas to the pan. If needed, add some of the chickpea cooking water to achieve the desired consistency of the curry. Simmer for 20-30 minutes. Adjust seasoning with salt.
  5. For the bhature, mix all-purpose flour with baking soda, a pinch of salt, and yogurt to form a soft dough. Cover and let it rest for at least 2 hours.
  6. Heat oil in a deep frying pan. Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each ball into a thin circle and deep fry one at a time until they puff up and turn golden brown.
  7. Serve the hot bhature with the spicy chole curry and enjoy.
Paneer Tikka

A vegetarian dish made with marinated paneer (Indian cottage cheese) and grilled to perfection.

Ingredients:
  1. Paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  2. Yogurt
  3. Ginger-garlic paste
  4. Spices (such as cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric, and red chili powder)
  5. Lemon juice
  6. Vegetables (such as bell peppers, onions, and tomatoes)
  7. Oil
  8. Salt
  9. Chaat masala (for garnish)
  10. Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
Cooking Instructions:
  1. Cut the paneer into cubes and chop the vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, spices, and salt to make the marinade.
  3. Add the paneer and vegetables to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Let it marinate for at least 1 hour in the refrigerator.
  4. Preheat your grill or pan on medium heat and lightly oil the surface.
  5. Thread the marinated paneer and vegetables onto skewers, alternating between them.
  6. Grill the skewers, turning occasionally, until the paneer is golden brown and the vegetables are cooked but still crisp, about 10-15 minutes.
  7. Sprinkle chaat masala and garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.
  8. Enjoy your Paneer Tikka with mint chutney or as a side dish.
