Butter Chicken

A popular Indian dish made with tender chicken cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce.

Ingredients:
  1. Chicken
  2. Butter
  3. Onion
  4. Garlic
  5. Ginger
  6. Tomatoes
  7. Heavy cream
  8. Spices (such as garam masala, turmeric, and chili powder)
  9. Salt
  10. Cilantro (for garnish)
Biryani

A flavorful rice dish made with aromatic spices, meat (such as chicken, mutton, or fish), and vegetables.

Ingredients:
  1. Rice
  2. Meat (chicken, mutton, or fish)
  3. Aromatic spices
  4. Vegetables
Paneer Tikka

A vegetarian dish made with marinated paneer (Indian cottage cheese) and grilled to perfection.

Ingredients:
  1. Paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  2. Yogurt
  3. Ginger-garlic paste
  4. Spices (such as cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric, and red chili powder)
  5. Lemon juice
  6. Vegetables (such as bell peppers, onions, and tomatoes)
  7. Oil
  8. Salt
  9. Chaat masala (for garnish)
  10. Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
Masala Dosa

A popular South Indian dish made with fermented rice and lentil batter, served with chutney and sambar.

Ingredients:
  1. Fermented rice
  2. Lentils
  3. Chutney
  4. Sambar
Gulab Jamun

A sweet Indian dessert made with milk solids (khoya) and soaked in sugar syrup.

Ingredients:
  1. Milk solids (khoya)
  2. Sugar
  3. Water
  4. Cardamom powder
  5. Rose water
  6. Saffron strands
  7. Ghee or oil (for frying)
Chole Bhature

A popular North Indian dish made with spicy chickpea curry (chole) and deep-fried bread (bhature).

Ingredients:
  1. Chickpeas
  2. Onion
  3. Tomatoes
  4. Ginger
  5. Garlic
  6. Green chilies
  7. Spices (such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala)
  8. Oil
  9. Baking soda
  10. All-purpose flour
  11. Yogurt
  12. Salt
